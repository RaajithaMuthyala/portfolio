My Journey

A cross-functional journey through healthcare, research, and technology — solving real problems with data and design.

Data Analyst – Purkayastha Lab for Health Innovation

Led the development of machine learning models for clinical data analysis and predictive modeling using algorithms such as logistic regression, random forests, and gradient boosting on structured EHR datasets. Built and maintained NLP pipelines to process unstructured clinical notes using spaCy, SciSpacy, and biomedical ontologies like SNOMED CT and UMLS. Designed scalable clinical decision support frameworks capable of handling large patient cohorts. Collaborated with clinical researchers to convert healthcare requirements into technical specifications and reproducible workflows by delivering analytical outputs through Python-based dashboards and Jupyter reports, supporting evidence-based clinical interventions.

Research Analyst – IU Bio Health Informatics Research Center at Indianapolis

Gathered and structured healthcare policy data from international sources including the Czech Republic, Montenegro, and Australia for global benchmarking studies. Utilized advanced SQL techniques such as complex JOINs, CTEs (Common Table Expressions), window functions (e.g., RANK(), ROW_NUMBER()), and nested subqueries for efficient data importing, normalization, and transformation across heterogeneous datasets. Performed rigorous data preprocessing, de-duplication, and validation steps to ensure compliance with research protocols and maintain high data integrity. Translated large-scale, multi-country datasets into analytical visualizations using R (ggplot2) and SPSS, highlighting key trends in healthcare delivery, access, and policy effectiveness to support strategic decision-making.

Data Analyst – Viatris

Executed analytical testing protocols using HPLC-MS instrumentation and managed data acquisition, processing, and reporting through Empower software, ensuring compliance with GLP standards. Constructed traceable, audit-ready datasets by integrating stability, dissolution, and bioavailability metrics for regulatory submissions to FDA (U.S.), EMA (EU), and CDSCO (India). Developed structured, QC-ready datasets and automated batch trend analysis using SAS procedures (PROC MEANS, PROC TRANSPOSE) and Excel VBA to support ongoing product evaluation. Collaborated with formulation and quality control teams to assess critical quality attributes (CQA) and optimize workflows for bioequivalence, stability, and release testing.

Data Analyst – MedBound

Processed and coded clinical documentation into structured datasets using ICD-10 and CPT standards, supporting analytics for outcomes research and care optimization. Assisted in participant recruitment and eligibility screening for clinical trials, contributing to enrollment tracking and protocol adherence. Utilized Medidata Rave to input, validate, and reconcile electronic case report forms (eCRFs), ensuring accuracy and compliance with data integrity protocols. Supported the preparation of Institutional Review Board (IRB) submission documents and developed foundational knowledge of Good Clinical Practice (GCP) and regulatory requirements in clinical research.
